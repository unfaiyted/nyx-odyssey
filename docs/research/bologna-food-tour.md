## üè™ Food Markets

### Quadrilatero (Il Quadrilatero)
- Bologna's historic food market district
- Medieval streets packed with food stalls, delis, fresh pasta shops
- **Key street:** Via Pescherie Vecchie ‚Äî "best lasagna in the world" per locals
- Adjacent to Piazza Maggiore

### Mercato di Mezzo
- Indoor food hall inside the Quadrilatero
- Fresh pasta, local wines, street food
- Great for lunch grazing

### Mercato delle Erbe
- Local produce market near the university district
- More local, less touristy than Quadrilatero
