## üèõÔ∏è Top Attractions

### Teatro Olimpico
- **What:** World's oldest surviving indoor theater (1580). Palladio's final masterpiece, completed by Scamozzi.
- **Why it's special:** The trompe l'oeil stage set creates an illusion of infinite streets receding into the distance. Still has the original Renaissance stage set ‚Äî the oldest surviving in the world.
- **Hours:** Tue‚ÄìSun 9:00‚Äì17:00 (last entry 16:30). Closed Mondays.
- **Price:** ‚Ç¨11 (includes Palazzo Chiericati). Combined Museum Card ‚Ç¨15.
- **Tips:** Go early to avoid tour groups. The perspective illusion is best from center seats. Occasional evening concerts ‚Äî check schedule. Photography allowed (no flash).
- **Location:** Piazza Matteotti

### Basilica Palladiana
- **What:** Palladio's first major public commission (1549). Medieval town hall wrapped in Renaissance marble loggias. Iconic green copper roof.
- **Why it's special:** The building that made Palladio's career. Upper floor hosts world-class art exhibitions. Rooftop terrace offers panoramic views.
- **Hours:** Varies by exhibition. Terrace open for events (check museicivicivicenza.it).
- **Price:** Varies by exhibition (‚Ç¨8‚Äì12 typical). Terrace often free or with drink purchase.
- **Tips:** Evening terrace aperitivo is magical. The square below hosts markets and events. The building is stunning at night when illuminated.
- **Location:** Piazza dei Signori

### Palazzo Chiericati (Civic Art Museum)
- **What:** Palladio-designed palace (1550), now the civic art museum (Museo Civico).
- **Collection:** Renaissance and Baroque paintings ‚Äî Veronese, Tintoretto, Tiepolo, Van Dyck. Also medieval and modern art.
- **Hours:** Tue‚ÄìSun 9:00‚Äì17:00. Closed Mondays.
- **Price:** ‚Ç¨11 (combined with Teatro Olimpico). Museum Card ‚Ç¨15.
- **Tips:** The building itself is the star. Gorgeous fa√ßade with double loggia. Don't miss the frescoed ceilings inside.
- **Location:** Piazza Matteotti (right next to Teatro Olimpico)

### Sanctuary of Monte Berico
- **What:** 15th-century hilltop basilica dedicated to the Virgin Mary. Pilgrimage site with panoramic views over Vicenza and the Veneto plain.
- **Getting there:** 
  - Walk up the Scalette (porticoed path with 150 arcades, ~20 min uphill ‚Äî great exercise)
  - Bus #18 from centro (~5 min)
  - Drive/taxi
- **Hours:** Church open daily 6:00‚Äì12:30, 14:30‚Äì19:00 (summer until 19:30)
- **Price:** Free
- **Must-see:** Veronese's "Supper of St. Gregory the Great" in the refectory. The panoramic terrace. The porticoed walk itself.
- **Tips:** Go at sunset for incredible views. Combine with Villa Valmarana and La Rotonda (all on the same hill). There's a restaurant (Da Biasio) nearby.

### Tabaccheria (Tobacconist)
The Italian convenience store. Marked by a blue "T" sign. Buy:

- Stamps, phone top-ups
- Lottery tickets, basic stationery
- Open early, close ~19:30. Some close for lunch.

### Water
Vicenza tap water is safe and excellent (Alpine-fed). Carry a refillable bottle. Public fountains (fontanelle) throughout centro provide free drinking water.

### Weekly Rhythms
- **Tuesday & Thursday mornings:** Market at Piazza delle Erbe (produce, cheese, flowers)
- **Saturday:** Larger market. Good day for day trips.
- **Sunday:** Most shops closed. Churches open. Restaurants open but book ahead. Best day for Monte Berico + villa circuit.
- **Monday:** Many museums closed. Good work/rest day.

### Things to Know
- **Siesta is real:** Most small shops close 12:30‚Äì15:30. Supermarkets stay open. Don't fight it ‚Äî rest or work during this time.
- **Dinner is late:** Before 19:30 you'll eat alone. Peak is 20:00‚Äì21:00.
- **Coffee rules:** Cappuccino only before 11 AM (locals will judge). Espresso any time. Don't ask for "to go."
- **Tipping:** Not expected. Round up or leave ‚Ç¨1‚Äì2 for exceptional service. Coperto (cover charge ‚Ç¨1‚Äì3) is normal.
- **Quiet hours:** Respect 13:00‚Äì15:00 and after 22:00. No loud music/noise.
- **Trash:** Vicenza has strict recycling (raccolta differenziata). Your landlord will explain the color-coded bins and pickup schedule.
- **AC:** Many Italian apartments have split-unit AC. June should be comfortable but can hit 30¬∞C+.

### June Weather

- **Avg low:** 16‚Äì19¬∞C (61‚Äì66¬∞F)
