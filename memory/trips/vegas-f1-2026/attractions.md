## üèúÔ∏è Day Trips

### Grand Canyon (West Rim)
- **Distance:** ~2.5 hours by car, or helicopter
- **Options:** Bus tours ($100-150), helicopter ($300-500), self-drive
- **Skywalk:** Glass-bottomed horseshoe walkway over the canyon, ~$30 + park entry ~$50
- **Worth it?** If you've never been, it's bucket-list worthy. West Rim is closest to Vegas.

### Hoover Dam
- **Distance:** 45 min from Strip
- **Dam Tour:** ~$30/person (guided interior tour)
- **Worth it?** Easy half-day trip. The engineering is impressive and the views from the bypass bridge are great. Can combine with lunch in Boulder City.

### Valley of Fire State Park
- **Distance:** ~1 hour from Strip
- **Cost:** $10/vehicle entry
- **Worth it?** HIGHLY recommended if you like nature/photography. Stunning red sandstone formations, petroglyphs, easy hikes. Best at sunrise/sunset. November weather is perfect for this.
- **Top spots:** Fire Wave Trail (easy, 1.5 miles), White Domes Loop (1 mile), Elephant Rock
