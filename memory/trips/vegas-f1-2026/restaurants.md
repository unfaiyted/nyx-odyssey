### Key Reservation Tips:
1. **Book 60-90 days ahead** (early September) for F1 week
2. **Carbone** is hardest ‚Äî set calendar reminder for exactly 30 days before
3. **OpenTable & Resy** are the main platforms; some restaurants use their own systems
4. **Concierge help** ‚Äî if staying at a nice hotel, their concierge may have connections
5. **Bar seating** is often available without reservations and many places serve full menus at the bar
6. **Late dining** (after 9:30pm) is easier to book ‚Äî and Vegas is a late-night city anyway
7. **Cancel early** if plans change ‚Äî no-show fees during F1 week could be $50-100/pp
